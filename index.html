Plays

6J Play

Middle Blocker broads out, decoying a hit towards Setter. Outside Hitter slides in towards Setter and hits a cut towards the other enemy Outside Hitter

10J Play

Middle Blocker decoys for a quick, but lets the set goes past to the Outside Hitter who hits it.

1 Pump, or 1J

Opposite Hitter will hit a 1J (No Tilt) set to the front of where the Middle Blocker would be hitting, instead of a regular back set towards the Opposite Hiter. This set could also be used for Middle Blockers to hit.

Middle Slide Delay/Slide Delay

3UP to antenna where the Opposite Hitter would normally spike. Opposite hitter will fake a quick, and Middle Blocker will then slide in and hit the 3UP set

3JW Decoy

3JW Arc set towards the Middle Blocker, but the Opposite Hitter will broad in and hits a cut, or wherever.

Oppo-Middle Soulswap

Opposite Hitter will shuffle places with the Middle Blocker, and the Middle Blocker will slide in and hi the ball. This can also be done in reverse.

5J Ground

Middle Blocker will walk towards the Setter, broading out and hits towards the Setters spot.

5J Pipe

2nd Outside in the backrow, will hit the 5J Pipe while the Middle Blocker decoys.

6UP Pipe

2D OUT will hit a set that lands short from the Outside Hitter in the front row, and can either aim for a wipe or get a clean hit.

Voldemort

A 4J(W/S) set will be for either the Opposite Hitter, 2nd Outside or the Middle Blocker to hit.

Outside Decoy Play (ODP)

Setter will set 8UP towards the Outside Hitter, the Outside Hitter will broad in and decoy a cut. The leftback hitter will jump from backrow and hit a short straight.

Head Tap Play

Setter will fake an ape and sets 2JW/2J to 2nd Outside right behind them
